Frequently Asked Questions

Clear, quick answers to help you get started with the BiteXR platform.

Do customers need to download an application? +
No. BiteXR uses advanced WebXR projection models, allowing customers to scan a table QR code and interact with 3D models directly inside Safari or Chrome on their smartphones.
How do we generate 3D models for our dishes? +
You can upload standard .glb or .usdz formats. If you do not have 3D models, BiteXR provides dynamic fallback graphics or hooks you up with our professional photogrammetry scanning partners.
Does it integrate with our existing restaurant POS system? +
Yes. BiteXR offers tableside KDS ordering dashboards, billing synchronizers, and order trackers that waitstaff or receptionists can run side-by-side with your legacy POS terminal.
